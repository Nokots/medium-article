Since 2004.

Dofus is a French MMORPG created by Ankama back in 2004, in Roubaix, France. Founded by Anthony Roux, Camiller Chafer and Emmanuel Darras, the studio started as a web agency before developing video games. As of 2021, Ankama is now a game developing studio, an animation studio, a publisher and a boardgames editor.

Multiple possibilites.

Dofus is a huge MMORPG. 17 years have passed since the game's launch, and developpers have added a lot of cool features.
In Dofus, you can do whatever you want, depending of what you like to do.
Some people enjoy farming mobs to loot rare items : you can do it !
Some people enjoy leveling up professions to make a lot of money : you can do it !
Some people enjoy playing against other players : you can do it !
